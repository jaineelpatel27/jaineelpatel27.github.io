Our Vision

Mission Statement:

To save the planet one textile at a time by providing information and resources to the public about practical ways to handle old clothing and reduce personal waste.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to see the rate at which textile waste accumulates in landfills slow down by educating the public about the issue and giving them ways to help and begin to manage their own personal textile waste levels responsibly. If the actions we take slow down this problem even a little, we would consider our initiative successful. We want this problem to be at the forefront of peopleâ€™s minds when they think about throwing an old piece of clothing away so they rethink their choice. We also want to see increased numbers of people showing up to our events so that we can spread our message to as many people as possible.
