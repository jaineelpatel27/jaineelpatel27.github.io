FAQ/Myths

Facts vs. Myths

Myth: Americans donate or recycle most of their used clothes.
Fact: Most Americans actually throw away most of their clothes because it is easier to.(planetaid)

Myth: Making clothing doesn't require that many harmful chemicals.
Fact: Production of textiles uses about 3500 different chemicals.(zerrin)

Q: Is it better to donate my clothes to charity or to recycle them?

A: It is best to recycle the clothes. This is because the root of the problem for countries who are struggling with this is the need to get clothes from local industries to reboot the economy within their own infrastructure. Simply giving clothes to people in need is good short term but does not solve the problem that we are addressing(planetaid.)

Q: How much of old clothes can be reused:

A: More than 60% can be reused. 35% can be recycled into washcloths and whatnot and that leaves 5% that should be discarded. (recylingwasteworld)

Q: How much has clothing production increased from 2000 to 2014?

A: It has doubled.(zerrin)

Q: How many companies are implementing plans to recycle better?

A: Around 11% of companies implement recycling strategies in production.(zerrin)
